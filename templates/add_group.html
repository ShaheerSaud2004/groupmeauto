How to Get Group Information
Step 1: Create a GroupMe Bot
  1. Go to GroupMe Developer Console
  2. Click "Create Bot"
  3. Select your group chat
  4. Give your bot a name (e.g., "Event Bot")
  5. Copy the Bot ID that's generated
Step 2: Find Your Group ID
  1. In your GroupMe app, go to the group chat
  2. Tap the group name at the top
  3. Scroll down to find the Group ID, or
  4. Use the GroupMe API to list your groups
Note: Make sure your bot is added to the group chat before using it to send messages.
